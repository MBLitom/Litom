Pricing

Pricing starts with scope, risk, and delivery responsibility.

MB Litom does not sell generic packages or cart checkout services. Work is proposed around a concrete outcome, milestone structure, and decision points.

Discovery and rescue audit

A short engagement to understand the codebase, business goal, constraints, and recommended next steps before committing to a larger build.

Scoped build milestone

A defined implementation phase with agreed deliverables, acceptance criteria, review points, and a payment request tied to the milestone.

Ongoing delivery support

Maintenance, iteration, integration work, and operational improvements after the foundation is stable and the priorities are clear.

Commercial model

Expect proposals, deposits, milestones, and delivery notes.

Each project starts with a written proposal that explains what will be built, what is excluded, what decisions are still open, and how payment requests align with delivery. Paysera Checkout is planned for later, but no payment integration is part of this public foundation.
