Process

A clear path from project request to delivered software.

The process is designed for serious B2B work: understand the business problem, define a proposal, build in milestones, and keep decisions visible.

  1. RequestYou share the goal, current systems, constraints, deadline pressure, and what success needs to look like.
  2. ProposalMB Litom writes the scope, assumptions, risks, milestone plan, deposit terms, and delivery responsibilities.
  3. Milestone buildWork moves through focused implementation phases with reviewable progress and practical technical choices.
  4. DeliveryThe milestone closes with working software, notes for operation, and a recommendation for the next stage.
