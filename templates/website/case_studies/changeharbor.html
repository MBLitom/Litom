What mattered

Architecture first, then usable business workflows.

The project required more than screens. It needed clear models, reliable background processing, admin visibility, change history, alerts, operational monitoring, and a maintainable Django foundation that could support continued iteration.

SaaS foundation

Billing operations

Invoice-oriented workflows, payment request thinking, plan seeding, and careful separation between commercial logic and future payment provider work.

Health checks, logs, worker visibility, screenshot monitoring, and practical debugging paths for delivery confidence.
