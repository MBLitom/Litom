Case study

ChangeHarbor proved the delivery discipline behind MB Litom.

ChangeHarbor was built as a practical SaaS system with production-minded architecture, invoice and billing workflows, monitoring, background jobs, and Dockerized development.

What mattered

Architecture first, then usable business workflows.

The project required more than screens. It needed clear models, reliable background processing, admin visibility, billing-adjacent flows, operational monitoring, and a maintainable Django foundation that could support continued iteration.

SaaS foundation

Django application structure, PostgreSQL, Redis, Docker, admin workflows, background jobs, and production-oriented settings.

Billing operations

Invoice-oriented workflows, payment request thinking, plan seeding, and careful separation between commercial logic and future payment provider work.

Monitoring and recovery

Health checks, logs, worker visibility, screenshot monitoring, and practical debugging paths for delivery confidence.
